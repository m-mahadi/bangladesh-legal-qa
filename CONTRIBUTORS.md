## How credit is preserved without an organization

The Hugging Face repository may be owned by one personal account while still
crediting the complete team through:

1. the author list on the main dataset card;
2. this contributors file;
3. the complete BibTeX citation in `CITATION.bib`;
4. the associated paper link when the paper becomes public;
5. release notes that retain the complete author order.

The owning account is the technical maintainer. It is not presented as the
sole creator of the dataset. The two research supervisors are included because
substantive supervision is part of the research contribution, while their role
is stated separately from hands-on dataset contribution.

## Contribution roles

The public release should add detailed, author-approved CRediT roles only after
the team agrees on them. This file currently distinguishes dataset contribution,
corresponding maintenance, and research supervision without inventing more
specific roles.
